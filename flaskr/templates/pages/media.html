Introduction

My love for Anime.

My love for anime began when I was in college. My best friend introduced me to this world, and it provided me with a unique and immersive experience. I was hooked from the first show he recommended!

Over the years, my passion for anime has only grown stronger. I've watched countless series and movies, discovering amazing stories and characters. My favorite genres include Yaoi, Drama and Shonen.

Some of my all-time favorite series include Jujutsu Kaisen, Kaiju No. 8, and I Want to Eat Your Pancreas. These shows have compelling storylines that blend action, emotion, and beautiful art. Whether it's the dark fantasy of Jujutsu Kaisen or the heartwarming drama of I Want to Eat Your Pancreas, anime continues to surprise and captivate me.

I look forward to the future of anime, filled with new and creative stories. Through this page, I hope to share my love of anime with you and highlight some of my favorite shows of the season. Enjoy!
